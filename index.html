About Us

We are Belle Event Decor, three girls with a love for decor. We have always enjoyed planning event for friends and family, Event Decor developed from being a hobby to passion. For almost a decade of navigating between professions. We have finally found our dream career and we want to pay it forward. we are extremely detail oriented, we love to create memories. Planning a Big Day? Not sure where to start and what is next? with a simple click or call, we can help you bring your event to life and provide the love and passion to make your dreams come true. Make us the best CHOICE

Belle Event Decor specializes in party decor. We design all type of events such as weddings, engagement parties, baby shower, bridal party, quinceanera, anniversary and much more and we go from elegant to sophisticated, Our attention to details is precise and our creativity endless. The most rewarding feeling we have is witnessing the satisfaction in the eyes of our clients. We will make your event special and unforgettable with extraordinary custom decor design. Belle Event Decor goal is to provide a stress-free experience and ensure all you have to do is show up. Leave the rest to us.
